Hungry for a Snack... try some Seattle Salmon Cookies!


Pat and his wife wanted to find a way to get their kids to eat healthier. They told their kids no dessert until they eat all their food, but it just didn't work.

While fishing for Chinook salmon one day, Pat was thinking about how much his kids liked cookies, and how much he liked salmon; and that's when Seattle Salmon cookies were born!

Pat and Penelope spent the next 8 months and their life's savings creating the perfect receipe. The best part is their kids can't get enough!
